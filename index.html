Welcome to Milroy Shoes

Milroy Shoes is a family-owned and operated business specializing in work shoes and leather products. They stock a complete line of work and western shoes and boots. They are an authorized dealer for the following companies: JUSTIN, ARIAT, DOUBLE H, CAROLINA, RED WING IRISH SETTER, THOROGOOD, SAS, BROOKS, and SKECHERS. Pinecraft Shoe also carries high quality, Amish-made leather products such as purses, belts, wallets, bags, and bridles.
